AI PING PONG GAME













Instructions

  1. First keep your laptop straight.
  2. Move yourself approximately 3 to 4 feets away from the laptop.
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist
  4. Now press the play button and again move 3 to 4 feets away from the the laptop.
  5. The red paddel is your paddel, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
